RxCheck : Applying to Becoming a Vendor

The Vendor Connect feature begins with the registration of an integration partner as an authorized vendor in RxConsole. The RxCheck v3.2 enhancement updated RxConsole to provide for an enhanced onboarding process, minimizing friction during the registration process.

The following subsections outline the process by which an individual or organization looking to become an authorized vendor in RxCheck can follow to begin the process.

This subsection describes the process and requirements needed to apply for registration as a vendor within RxCheck.

  1. Collect the following information for your application as an authorized RxCheck Integration Vendor.

    1. Vendor Name

    2. Vendor Street Address

    3. Vendor Email

    4. Vendor Phone Number

    5. Hosting Details

      1. Where is the SRS server located?

      2. Are the SRS servers being accessed from outside the US?

      3. Cloud Details

    6. Vendor Contact First Name

    7. Vendor Contact Last Name

    8. Vendor Contact Email

    9. Vendor Contact Phone Number

A detailed explanation for each piece of required information is provided in the table following these steps.

  1. Send the collected information to the RxCheck team in an email at the following address: PDMPTTAC@iir.org

  2. Your information will be reviewed for completeness.

    1. If everything is complete and correct, you or your organization will be registered as an authorized vendor in the RxCheck interstate data-sharing network.

    2. If any information is missing or incomplete, you will receive an email explaining what needs to be corrected to facilitate your registration.

  3. After being registered as an authorized vendor in the RxCheck network, your Vendor Name will appear in the dropdown for a PDMP admin to select as the vendor for an authorized healthcare entity in RxConsole.

  4. As part of the onboarding process as an authorized vendor, a vendor admin account will be created for you.

Field

Description

Vendor Name

The name of the organization that is providing for HCE EHR integration. This will also be the name that will appear in the dropdown for the PDMP administrator to select setting up a HCE.

Vendor Street Address

The street address that the vendor physically operates out of and is listed as the official business address.

Vendor Email

The general business email address that goes to a support center or an individual for triage.

Vendor Phone Number

The general phone number to reach a vendor representative.

Where is the SRS server located?

This can be listed as one of the three following options:

  • Vendor IT Infrastructure

  • Private Cloud

  • Government Cloud

Are the SRS servers being accessed from outside the US?

This can only be answered as either: Yes or No .

Cloud Details

This is an open field but can include any information that is relevant to your SRS server hosting. Examples include hosting service, server hardware specifics, SRS version, etc.

Vendor Contact First Name

The first name of the vendor contact responsible for the server hosting the SRS.

Vendor Contact Last Name

The last name of the vendor contact responsible for the server hosting the SRS.

Vendor Contact Email

The email address of the vendor contact responsible for the server hosting the SRS.

Vendor Contact Phone Number

The phone number of the vendor contact responsible for the server hosting the SRS.
