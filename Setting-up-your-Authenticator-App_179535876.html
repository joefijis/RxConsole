RxCheck : Setting up your Authenticator App

Created by Joseph Fibich, last modified on Dec 22, 2025

If the user previously set up authentication in a mobile authenticator app, they should delete the existing RxConsole account entry in that authenticator app before scanning the new QR code to avoid duplicate entries.

Google Authenticator

  1. Launch the Google Authenticator app on your mobile device.

  1. Press the + button.

  1. Press the camera icon to scan a QR code.

  1. Scan the QR code in the RxConsole email.

Duo Authenticator

  1. Launch the Duo Mobile Authenticator app on your mobile device.

  1. Press the + Add button.

  1. Press the Use QR Code button.

  1. Scan the QR code in the RxConsole email.

Microsoft Authenticator

  1. Launch the Microsoft Authenticator app on your mobile device.

  1. Press the + icon.

  1. Select Work or school account.

  1. Press the Scan QR Code option.
