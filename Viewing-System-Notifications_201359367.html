RxCheck : Viewing System Notifications

Created by Joseph Fibich on Jan 13, 2026

  1. After logging into the RxConsole application, a PDMP Administrator will be able to see the Notification(s) icon in the top right corner under their name.

  1. Clicking on the Notification(s) icon, will display the message relating to the notification, if a notification exists.

The number of notifications will appear between the “Bell” icon and the word “Notification” on the button.

  1. Clicking on the blue circle X button will minimize the notification.

Notifications will be shown for:

  • Heartbeat Notifications

  • New MOU Worksheets

  • Scheduled Maintenance Events

  • Certificate Expirations

  • Release Updates
