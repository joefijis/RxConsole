RxCheck : Viewing Patient and Provider Prescription Data in Analytical Insights

Created by Joseph Fibich, last modified on Dec 23, 2025

  1. Click the area graph icon on the left-hand side of the screen to access the RxCheck Dashboard.

  1. Click on the Patient or Provider button located in the top middle of the page, depending on the information you would like to display.

  1. Click on the filter button in the top right corner.

  1. After clicking the filter button, you can view the search filters.

  1. The Incoming and Outgoing buttons allow you to filter the request type.

The Incoming option is selected and displayed by default.

  1. Use the Time Range dropdown to select a period you want to display.

  1. Prescription information can be searched by County or Zip Code.

To search by County

  1. Click on the County radio button.

The County button is selected by default.

  1. Click on the downward-facing arrow for the filter titled “County” to reveal a list of counties.

  1. Select your County(ies) by:

    1. Scrolling through the dropdown options and checking the box, or

    2. Typing in the search bar to filter results.

  1. Click the Search button to display prescription counts for patients based on the counties selected.

  1. Results are displayed on the map with the user’s state highlighted green and the connected states in orange.

How to search by Zip Code

  1. Click on the Zip Code radio button.

  1. Enter the zip code and the desired distance in miles around the zip code to be searched.

  1. Click the Search button to display prescription counts for patients based on the zip code and range entered.

  1. Results are displayed on the map with the user’s state highlighted green and the connected states in orange.

Click on the Reset Filters button to return all selections to the default values.
